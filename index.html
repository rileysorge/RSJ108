How to make a thick Tweet ...

Assembly time: 5 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to make an effective tweets that engage your followers? Look no further- the thick tweet is here to save the day!

  • Things You'll Need

  • A Twitter account
  • Something you want to tweet about
  • A picture relating to said thing you want to tweet about
  • A creative caption
  • A cool hashtag!!
  • Maybe some followers

  1. Make a Twitter account

    You can't tweet without a Twitter account!

  2. Get to typing!

    Write the content of the tweet and play around with what sounds best.

    Twitter was created in 2006.

  3. Adding a photo.

    Once you've decided on the wording and a fun hashtag, it's time to add the photo.

  4. Read over the tweet.

    Double check your tweet to make sure it makes sense and there's no grammatical errors.

  5. Click "tweet"

    Finally, click the "tweet" button to post it to Twitter.
